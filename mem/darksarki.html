Sarkie Sarangay

  • Birthday: November 16, 1982

Sarkie Sarangay is the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist of Silent Sanctuary, a Filipino rock band
known for its unique blend of classical strings and rock music. With his distinct voice and emotive
delivery, Sarkie has played a pivotal role in shaping the band's sound, contributing to many of their
hit songs that explore themes of love, heartbreak, and longing. His songwriting, often characterized
by heartfelt lyrics and melodic hooks, resonates deeply with the band's dedicated fanbase, making
him a central figure in the Philippine music scene.
