Antoni Inacay

  • Name: Antoni Josef Inacay
  • Age: 36
  • Birthday: December 4, 1987
  • Occupation: Cellist
  • Nethworth: 1M

Antoni Josef Inacay is a talented cellist and member of the Filipino band Silent Sanctuary, celebrated
for his contributions to the group's distinctive sound, which combines pop and classical elements.
His skillful cello performances enhance the band's melodic richness, while their heartfelt lyrics
resonate deeply with fans. Antoni's artistry plays a crucial role in shaping Silent Sanctuary's identity
within the OPM (Original Pilipino Music) scene.
