Kim Mirandilla-Ng

  • Name: Kim Mirandilla-Ng/Kim Carl Mirandilla
  • Age: 34
  • Birthday: February 27, 1990
  • Occupation: Vocalist
  • Nethworth: Unknown

Kim Mirandilla-Ng is the talented vocalist and one of the founding members of the Filipino band
Silent Sanctuary. Known for her powerful and emotive voice, she brings a unique blend of passion
and artistry to the band's music, which often explores themes of love and heartbreak. Kim's
contributions not only elevate the band's sound but also connect deeply with fans, making her an
integral part of Silent Sanctuary's identity and success in the OPM (Original Pilipino Music) scene.
